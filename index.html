Biography

Surroundings are a band with a lot to say. Following the hugely exciting response to the double singles 'Jane' and 'Elizabeth', Surroundings have seized the attention of the world of heavy music - now they intend on keeping it.

The band's signature sound - technical riffs, widescreen atmospherics, crushingly intimate vocals - have been paired with a renewed focus on huge choruses, watertight structuring and grooves that beg to be heard by your fellow commuters.

'Jane' and 'Elizabeth' have given the band's dedicated fanbase a small taste of what to expect from their upcoming album. The tracks, each accompanied by an evocative short film, showcased the true arrival of Australia's next world-class heavy act to tens of thousands of viewers. Though anyone who has seen the band live in the past year might tell you that Surroundings have already been there for some time.

Shows with powerhouses Northlane, Confession and Saviour, selling out Perth's premier venues have established Surroundings among Perth's top live acts. Expect to see the crowd losing themselves in atmospherics one minute and losing their shoes in the pit the next.

The band will bring their show to the rest of Australia following the release of the new album.
