Biography

Formed in 2011 by five close friends, Surroundings are a melodic hardcore/metal band from Perth, Western Australia. Fuelled by a passion to create refreshing music within the genre, Surroundings have crafted a unique blend of heavy guitar work and ambience not too dissimilar from the world of post-rock.
